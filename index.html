About the Clinic

Healthcare
Built for
Afienya

Oasis Medical Clinic is a trusted healthcare facility located off First Bridge Road, Mataheko-Afienya, Greater Accra. We combine modern clinical standards with genuine, compassionate care — offering everything from general consultations to specialist clinics.

Rated "Very Serene Medical Environment" by our patients — we believe that healing begins in the atmosphere. Our skilled team is committed to your wellbeing, Monday through Saturday, 8 AM to 8 PM, with an after-hours line always available.
