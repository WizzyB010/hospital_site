Our Story

A Landmark of Healthcare in the Afienya Community

Founded with a deep commitment to community health, Oasis Medical Clinic has served the Afienya area for over a decade — offering accessible, high-quality medical care to families across the Greater Accra Region.

We blend modern medical technology with warm, personalized care — because every patient deserves to feel seen, heard, and healed.
